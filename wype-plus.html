The professional's choice for a flawless dry.

The wype+™ is engineered for detailers who refuse to compromise. At 1600 GSM and 100×80 cm, it absorbs faster, covers more panel in a single pass, and leaves zero watermarks — on paintwork, glass, or trim.

"If it doesn't deliver a showroom finish, return it. No questions asked."

Most microfibre cloths on the market sit between 300–900 GSM. The wype+™ is woven to a certified 1600 GSM — nearly double the density of anything you'll find in a supermarket. That density means dramatically more fibre contact per square centimetre, pulling water off the panel instead of just pushing it around. One pass. Done.

Standard microfibre towels have raw-cut or overlock-stitched edges that can snag and leave micro-scratches in your clear coat. The wype+™ uses a proprietary silk-bound edge — soft, flat, and completely snag-free. Safe on fresh paint, ceramic coatings, and vinyl wraps.

At 100×80 cm, the wype+™ is one of the largest drying towels available. Folded into quarters you get a 50×40 cm working surface — enough to cover a full bonnet in a single swipe. Less time drying. Less risk of recontamination from repeated contact. More time enjoying a clean car.

Paintwork, glass, plastic trim, chrome, alloys — the wype+™ handles it all without streaking or lint transfer. The split-fibre construction grips and lifts water rather than spreading it, leaving a crystal-clear finish on every surface in one pass.

The wype+™ is manufactured to strict European quality standards. Every batch is tested for GSM accuracy, fibre integrity, and colourfastness before it ships. No cutting corners, no inconsistent quality. What you order today is exactly what you'll get in your third reorder.

Machine wash at 30°C on a gentle cycle. No fabric softener — it clogs the fibres. No tumble drying above 40°C. Wash separately from cotton towels or clothing. Done correctly, the wype+™ retains full performance for 300+ washes. That's years of professional use from a single towel.
