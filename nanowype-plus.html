The softest contact. The sharpest finish.

The NanoWype+™ is not a standard microfibre towel. The nano wave weave structure creates thousands of micro-channels at the fibre level, actively drawing water off the surface via capillary action. It lifts more water with less contact, dries three times faster than flat-pile alternatives, and leaves glass and paintwork completely residue-free.

Built for ceramic coatings, PPF, fresh paint and precision glass work. If you care about the finish, this is the cloth.

Nano wave is a structured, undulating weave pattern engineered at the fibre level. Unlike flat-pile microfibre, nano wave fibres create thousands of micro-channels that actively draw water into the cloth via capillary action rather than relying on passive absorption. The result is dramatically faster water lift with a fraction of the drag force on your paintwork. This is why it is safe on fresh ceramic coatings, soft clear coats, and hydrophobic surfaces where any friction risk is unacceptable.

Both are exceptional but built for different jobs. The NanoWype+™ at 1200 GSM uses nano wave technology to maximise water lift with ultra-soft contact. It is ideal for coated paintwork, glass, windows and anyone who prioritises a completely scratch-risk-free finish. The wype+™ at 1600 GSM delivers sheer maximum absorption for fast, high-volume drying. Many detailers use both: NanoWype+™ for the final panel and glass wipe, wype+™ for initial drying on larger panels.

Standard microfibre towels use raw-cut or overlock-stitched edges that can catch and drag across your clear coat. The NanoWype+™ uses a proprietary silk-bound edge. Completely flat, snag-free, and safe on fresh paint, ceramic coatings, vinyl wraps and glass surrounds. Not a single fibre or stitch to catch on trim, seals or panel gaps.

At 90×70 cm, the NanoWype+™ is one of the largest drying towels available. Folded into quarters you have a 45×35 cm working surface, enough to cover a full bonnet in a single swipe. Less time drying, less contact with the panel, less risk of contamination from repeated passes. The nano wave construction means it wicks water so efficiently that a single fold is typically enough for an entire car.

The nano wave structure was specifically developed for coated surfaces. The undulating weave glides over hydrophobic paintwork with minimal friction. Water beads are lifted off the surface without dragging. Safe on all ceramic coating brands, PPF, and freshly applied sealants. Unlike flat-pile cloths, the wave pattern floats over the coating surface rather than pressing flat against it, which dramatically reduces the chance of disturbing a fresh application.

Paint drying: The primary use. The nano wave draws water off the panel with minimal contact, leaving a flawless finish on standard paint, ceramic-coated, PPF-wrapped and freshly sealed vehicles.

Glass and windscreens: The nano wave structure lifts water without smearing, giving a streak-free, crystal-clear result on windscreens, side windows, rear glass and mirrors in a single pass. No glass cleaner needed for water removal.

Wax and sealant removal: The ultra-fine nano fibre pile is soft enough to buff away wax and sealant residue from paintwork without scratching. The structured weave picks up product without spreading it.

Interior detailing: Safe on dashboards, door cards, leather trim and screen surrounds. The nano pile lifts dust and product residue without abrasion.

Wheel and rim finishing: Once the wheel is cleaned, the NanoWype+™ dries alloys, spokes and calipers quickly without leaving lint.

Post-spray detailer buffing: The nano wave pile is ideal for buffing a quick detailer, maintenance spray or ceramic spray to a high gloss without marring.

Machine wash at 30°C on a gentle cycle. No fabric softener as it clogs the nano fibres and destroys the wave structure. No tumble drying above 40°C. Wash separately from cotton towels. Follow this routine and your NanoWype+™ retains full performance for 300+ washes.
