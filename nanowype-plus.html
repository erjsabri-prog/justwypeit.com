Standard microfibre uses a flat pile where fibres sit parallel to the surface. Nano wave fibres are woven into a structured undulating pattern, creating micro-channels between the waves. These channels actively draw water in via capillary action, meaning far less pressure is needed to lift water off the panel. The nano wave structure can wick water up to three times faster than flat-pile alternatives of equivalent GSM. Faster drying with a lighter touch and less risk to the finish.
