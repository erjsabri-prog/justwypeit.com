Our Story

Built from hard work.
Driven by passion.

Two delivery drivers. One shared dream. The belief that quality should never be out of reach.

It started with early mornings and long routes. We were just two mates driving for Amazon, but sharing something bigger: a genuine love for cars and the drive to build something of our own.

Where it all began

The journey

How wype® came to life

01

The Grind

Every spare pound we earned, we saved. No shortcuts, no handouts. Just discipline, long hours, and a shared vision. While others were spending, we were planning. Late-night talks about dream cars, detailing routines, and what it would feel like to own something we had truly worked for.

Discipline Saving Vision
02

The Dream Car Moment

Eventually, that moment came. We each bought our dream cars, not just as a reward, but as proof of what consistency achieves. But once we had them, we realised something frustrating: keeping them clean to the standard we wanted was not easy. The products available simply were not good enough.

300+ wash cycles tested. 12 months of R&D. Two founders who refused to compromise.

03

The Problem

We tried product after product. Cheap materials that scratched paintwork. Poor absorption that needed endless passes. Cloths that degraded after just a few washes. We kept spending money replacing bad products instead of investing in quality ones. That did not sit right with us.

No scratches ✕ Poor durability ✕ Cheap finish ✕
04

The Decision

So we built something better. What started as a simple idea became a mission: a premium microfibre cloth that actually does the job, whether you are a professional detailer or just someone who takes pride in their car. No more compromises. No more wasted money.

Zero scratch guarantee ✓ 300+ washes ✓ Streak-free ✓
