Product & Care

Machine wash at 30°C on a gentle cycle. Do not use fabric softener — it coats the fibres and reduces absorbency. Do not tumble dry above 40°C. Wash separately from cotton towels or clothing. Follow this routine and your wype+™ will perform at full capacity for 300+ washes.

Yes. At 100×80 cm and 1600 GSM, the wype+™ has enough surface area and absorption to dry most cars in a single pass, especially when used with a quality drying aid. For larger vehicles like vans or SUVs, we recommend the 2-towel bundle.

Yes. The anti-snag silk-bound hem and ultra-soft 1600 GSM pile are safe on ceramic coatings, paint protection film, vinyl wraps, and fresh paintwork. No snagging, no abrasion, no damage to your coating.

The wype+™ has a dual-pile construction. The longer, plusher side pulls water off the panel fast. The shorter, denser side is ideal for a finishing buff on glass and trim — leaving a streak-free surface on every pass.

No. The wype+™ performs without a drying aid. That said, combining it with a quick detailer or drying spray enhances the gloss and helps it glide even more smoothly across the panel — especially in warm weather.
