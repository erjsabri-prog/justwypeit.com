Standard delivery is £3.99 on a single towel. Order 2 or more and delivery is completely free. No hidden charges at checkout.

Product & Care

Machine wash at 30°C on a gentle cycle. Do not use fabric softener, it coats the fibres and reduces absorbency. Do not tumble dry above 40°C. Wash separately from cotton towels or clothing. Follow this routine and your MicroWype+™ will perform at full capacity for 300+ washes.

Yes. At 40×40 cm, the MicroWype+™ has excellent absorption for panel-by-panel drying, especially when used with a quality drying aid. For full-car drying in fewer passes, we recommend the 2-towel bundle.

Yes. The anti-snag silk-bound hem and ultra-soft pile are safe on ceramic coatings, paint protection film, vinyl wraps, and fresh paintwork. No snagging, no abrasion, no damage to your coating.

The MicroWype+™ has a dual-pile construction. The longer, plusher side pulls water off the panel fast. The shorter, denser side is ideal for a finishing buff on glass and trim, leaving a streak-free surface on every pass.
